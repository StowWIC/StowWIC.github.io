Stow Women In Computing Hack Club

About Stow WIC

Our mission is to create a supportive community that empowers women to pursue careers in computing. We do so through to learning, creating, and attending tech events together!

Mrs.Whitmer ~ Advisor/Founder

Mrs. Whitmer is a teacher at Stow-Munroe Falls High School and serves as the Stow WIC Club Advisor as well as Senior Class Advisor, and Business Professionals of America Advisor. Her teaching experience since the year 2000 includes a variety of business and computing courses such as AP Computer Science A, AP Computer Science Principles, C# Programming, Java Programming, Introduction to Coding, and Web Design (HTML/CSS). She also teaches a two-year Career-Technical Education program called "Programming & Software Development" where students can earn both college and high school credit.
